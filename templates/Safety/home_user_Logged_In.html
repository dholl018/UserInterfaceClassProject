Virginia Statistics

In viewing the impact of the unfunded CSHO positions through the lens of VOSH hazard instances (i.e., each hazard instance corrected represents an occupational hazard that was corrected and from which employees were protected), there has also been a steady decline over the last several calendar years, which represents increased risk for Virginia employees and employers. The average number of instances (16,534) for the most recent three (3) years of 2016 to 2018 is 29.2% below that for the period 2010 to 2012 (23,339).
